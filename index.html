Privacy Policy for Hello Satellite

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Captured images and collections are saved only on the user's device and are never uploaded to external servers.

The app accesses satellite imagery provided by third-party data sources. No user data is transmitted in this process.

No account registration or login is required to use this app.

The app does not use advertising SDKs or analytics tools that track user behavior.
